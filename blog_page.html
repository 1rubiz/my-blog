TECHNOLOGY:
 Technological Development In Recent Times

Febuary 28th, 2022.

In recent times, the impact technology has been felt more than ever mostly due to the social distancing rule imposed on the world by the present pandemic (covid 19). Areas of our daily with the most Technological impact includes:

  • Education:

During the pandemic, schools were closed world wide and it resulted in an interuption in the school calender. Most schools turned to unline learning to continue their lessons. Digital platforms that connected people together were utilized along side various devices such as phones, tablets, laptops etc . This ensured the continous flow of learning.

  • Communication:

Humans naturally are known as social organisms and therefore need to constantly keep in touch with each other. This also was impeded by the pandemic. The lock-down and social distancing hindered normal contact, but with the aid of our telecommunication devices, we could keep tabs on family and friends all around the world.

  • Businesses:

Most businesses all around the world sufferded due lack of lack of employees appraerance at work. However most business meetings were held online across various platforms. Those businesses that had to close down turned to freelancing jobs online.

  • Social Coverages:

Over the years, most ceremonial pictures and videos were covered by camera-men who were on the field. This however had several range limitations but these limitations are now being aided with drones providing arial/vantage view.
